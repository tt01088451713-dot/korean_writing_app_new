Korean Writing App – Privacy Policy (Draft)
===========================================

1. Information We Collect
Korean Writing App does not require user registration and does not collect any personally identifiable information.
Only learning progress and app settings are stored locally on the user’s device.  
No data is transmitted to external servers.

2. Purpose of Information Use
The locally stored data (learning progress and settings) is used solely to improve user convenience and maintain learning continuity.
No data is shared with other users or third parties.

3. Retention and Deletion of Information
All data will be automatically deleted when the app is uninstalled.  
Since the data is stored locally on the device, users do not need to take any additional action to delete it.

4. Provision to Third Parties
This app does not share, sell, or entrust any collected data to third parties under any circumstances.

5. User Rights
Users can delete all stored data by uninstalling the app or by using the reset function within the app.

6. Contact for Privacy Inquiries
Name: Kim Sang-Tae  
Organization: KST Lingua Studio  
Email: support-kst@naver.com

7. Additional Information
This app is an educational tool based on the textbook “쉽게 배우는 한국어 쓰기 교본 (Easy Korean Writing Textbook)”  
and does not request or store any personal information from learners.
