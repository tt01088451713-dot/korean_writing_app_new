App Name: Korean Writing App
Subtitle: Based on “Easy Korean Writing Textbook”

[About this app]
"Korean Writing App" is a Hangul learning application based on the textbook
“쉽게 배우는 한국어 쓰기 교본 (Easy Korean Writing Textbook)”.
It helps learners master Korean writing step by step — from consonants and vowels
to full syllables, words, and sentences.

[Main Features]
• Stroke-order based writing practice with interactive animations
• Practice for left-right combined syllables (가~하, 갸~햐, etc.)
• Pronunciation playback via built-in TTS
• Multilingual interface: Korean, English, Chinese, Japanese, Vietnamese, and more
• Designed for both mobile and desktop environments (Windows, Android, iOS)

[Recommended For]
• International students learning Korean
• Children from multicultural families
• Korean language teachers and tutors
• Beginners preparing for TOPIK
